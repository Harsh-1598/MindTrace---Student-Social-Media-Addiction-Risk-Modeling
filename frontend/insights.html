Dataset Overview

The model was trained on a dataset of 700+ diverse students to learn the correlations between social media habits and mental wellbeing.

  • R² Score: 0.9597 - The model explains approximately 96% of the variance in addiction scores.
  • Target variable represents a continuous addiction score from 2 to 9, scaled to 0-100% for usability.
  • Applies Outlier Handling using IQR Clipping, Logarithmic & Square Root feature engineering, and PCA dimensionality reduction.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How does the AI calculate my score?
A: It uses a trained Machine Learning model (Lasso Regression Pipeline) that compares your inputs to patterns seen across hundreds of other students' habits, outputting a severity score.
Q: Are my predictions saved to a server?
A: No. All prediction history and user accounts are stored entirely locally in your browser's database (localStorage) to ensure absolute privacy.
Q: Is this a clinical diagnosis?
A: No. This tool provides educational insights based on statistical patterns. It should not replace professional medical or psychological evaluation.
