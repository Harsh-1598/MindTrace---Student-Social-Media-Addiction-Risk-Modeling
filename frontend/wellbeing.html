🌱 Digital Wellbeing Guide

Curated resources to balance your screen time.

⏰ The 20-20-20 Rule

Every 20 minutes spent using a screen, try to look away at something that is 20 feet away from you for a total of 20 seconds. This significantly reduces digital eye strain.

🛌 Screen-Free Bedrooms

Keep your charger outside your bedroom to prevent doom-scrolling before sleep. Relying on an old-school alarm clock drastically improves sleep architectures.

🗑️ App Detox

Remove social media algorithms off your home screen. Only allow them inside specific app folders to create high friction to access them unconsciously.

📞 Seek Connection

Digital spaces often create a false sense of connection. Prioritize face-to-face meetups, physical study groups, and outdoor athletic engagements to ground yourself.
